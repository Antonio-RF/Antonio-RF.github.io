Strong Password Generator

This is a project developed during the summer vacation of 2024/2025 and consists of a strong password generator. It includes various options related to password creation and encryption, such as: checking strong passwords, generating a random strong password, generating an encrypted strong password, decrypting a password, and encrypting a password with a specific key.
